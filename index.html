Calculation Methodology

Z-Score Formula

Z = ((Value / M)L − 1) / (L × S)
L
Lambda (Box-Cox power transformation)
M
Mu (median reference value)
S
Sigma (coefficient of variation)

Percentile Calculation

Percentile is derived from the Z-score using the standard normal cumulative distribution function.
